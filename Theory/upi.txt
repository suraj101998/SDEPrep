1. Key Features of UPI
Instant Fund Transfer: Money can be transferred 24/7 without requiring bank details, only using a UPI ID.
Multiple Bank Accounts: One UPI app can handle multiple bank accounts.
QR Code Payments: Supports scanning QR codes for payments.
Two-Factor Authentication: Secure transactions using MPIN.
Auto-Pay & Recurring Payments: UPI 2.0 introduced auto-pay features for bills, subscriptions, etc.

5. Use Cases of UPI
Peer-to-peer (P2P) payments.
Bill payments (electricity, water, mobile recharges).
E-commerce & online shopping.
Business-to-business (B2B) transactions.
